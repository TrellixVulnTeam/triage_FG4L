3.52 Derived Element
3.52.1 Semantics
A derived element is one that can be computed from another one, but that is
shown for clarity or that is included for design purposes even though it adds no
semantic information.
3.52.2 Notation
A derived element is shown by placing a slash (/) in front of the name of the
derived element, such as an attribute or a rolename.
-----------

This is especially usefull on associations and attributes.

The "derived" is a standard tagged value defined for ModelElement.
